What we do

We Offer Quality Professional Services

Orsaroth offers a variety of human resource services and solutions with the aim of addressing some aspects of people management and development

Optimising return on investment

Comprehensive HR Audit, Advisory and Policies:

  • HR Audit: Conduct an audit to determine your current HR practices to identify gaps and articulate improvement opportunities.

  • HR Advisory: Provide HR Advisory support to your business and team.

  • Labour Law Advisory: Provide guidance and assist in reviewing all HR related documents to ensure compliance with the Labour Law.
